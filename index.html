Why Certified Used

What "Certified" Actually Means

✅ Tree Integrity Verified

Every saddle is tested for tree integrity before listing. David presses on each side of the fork and checks for any flex, creak, or movement. A broken tree is a deal-breaker and never enters this inventory.

🔍 Leather Condition Documented

Fender creases, seat jockeys, and rigging areas are inspected for cracking or dry rot. Surface wear is noted honestly. Every condition description reflects what David would tell a buyer face to face.

🔧 Hardware Inspected

Dee rings checked for cracks or deformation. Billet straps tested for dry rot. Rigging hardware verified. Anything questionable is noted or replaced before a saddle is listed.

🤝 Honestly Priced

David prices saddles at market value based on actual condition, maker reputation, and current demand in the NRHA and NCHA community. No wishful pricing. No padding for negotiation. The listed price is a real price.

Want List & Consignment

Don't see what you need? Tell David your discipline, maker preference, seat size, and price range — he'll reach out when something matching comes through. He also accepts quality performance saddles on consignment.
