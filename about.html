Who We Are

Buying a used performance saddle online usually means trusting a photo and a stranger. We built CertifiedUsedSaddles.com to remove that gamble. Every saddle listed here is inventory David Solum has personally inspected and owns. Nothing is drop-shipped, brokered sight-unseen, or listed on someone else's word.

With more than 40 years in the western performance world — across reining, cutting, cow horse, and ranch disciplines — David knows what separates a saddle worth riding from one that just photographs well. That experience is the whole point of the site.

What "Certified" Means Here

It means a real person has had the saddle in hand. Trees are checked. Leather, rigging, and stitching are evaluated. Condition is described honestly, and additional photos or details are available on request for any saddle. When you call about a saddle, you're talking to the person who inspected it.
