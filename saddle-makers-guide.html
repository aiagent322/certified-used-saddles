Tier 1 — Custom Artisan & Elite Performance Makers
Superior Saddlery — Andy Maschke
Greenville, Texas  ·  Active Production
ReiningCow HorseRanchCutting Active Production

Andy Maschke founded Superior Saddlery after training as a saddlemaker in Europe before relocating to Arizona and later establishing the shop in Greenville, Texas. The shop's SYMMETREES system uses hardwood tree cores fully encapsulated in fiberglass, which eliminates the moisture warping that affects traditional rawhide-covered wood trees.

The Ranch Rider and Ranch Rider Deluxe lines are in David's current inventory. Signature models including Casey Deary, Craig Schmersal, Jason Vanlandingham, and Mandy McCutcheon are in David's current inventory. New Superior Saddlery saddles are available through David Solum.

Donn Leson
Grants Pass, Oregon  ·  Used Market Only — Deceased
Reining Used Market Only — Deceased

Donn Leson built reining saddles in Grants Pass, Oregon. He is no longer living and production has permanently ceased, making his saddles available only through the used market.

Dale Harwood — Trail's End Saddlery
Shelley, Idaho  ·  Retired 2018 — Collector Market
RanchRopingWorking Retired 2018 — Collector Market

Dale Harwood received a National Heritage Fellowship from the National Endowment for the Arts. He opened his first saddle shop in Idaho Falls, Idaho in 1961 and built the last two saddles of his career in 2018. He made his own wooden saddle trees from scratch, processed his own rawhide, and hand-stamped the leather entirely by hand.

Circle Y

RanchReiningBarrelTrailShow Active — Est. 1960

Circle Y was founded by Leland Tucker in Yoakum, Texas in 1960. The company produces western saddles across multiple disciplines and uses a Flex2 tree design.

Genuine Billy Cook
2632 Koller Road, Sulphur, Oklahoma 73086  ·  Active — Est. early 1950s
RanchRopingReiningBarrelTrail Active — Est. early 1950s

Billy Cook began building saddles in Greenville, Texas in the early 1950s before relocating to Sulphur, Oklahoma, where the company remains today at 2632 Koller Road, Sulphur, OK 73086. The company is now owned by Adam Trenk, an Arizona horseman and attorney. Note: saddles stamped "Billy Cook Saddlery Greenville, TX" are a separate, mass-produced line — genuine Billy Cook saddles are stamped "Billy Cook Maker USA Genuine Sulphur, OK."
