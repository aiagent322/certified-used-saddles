Understanding Cow Horse Saddles

The Three-Event Demand

NRCHA competition demands a saddle that handles reining patterns, fence work, and boxing cattle — all in a single competition day. The cow horse saddle sits between a reining saddle (too flat for cow work) and a cutting saddle (too deep for patterns). Bob's Custom Lady Cowhorse is a purpose-designed crossover build — brand new in this inventory, which means no previous tree stress and full manufacturer quality.

Why Brand New Matters Here

The Lady Cowhorse saddles in this inventory are brand new — never ridden, tree never stressed, leather never compressed by use. At $4,995 for a Bob's Custom new build, this represents below-retail pricing through David's inventory rather than a retailer markup. The certification process still applies: David has handled and inspected every saddle before listing.
