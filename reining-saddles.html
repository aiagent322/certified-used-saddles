Reining Knowledge

Understanding Reining Saddles

What to Look For

Flat seat, minimal horn, in-skirt rigging, round skirts. Best from proven makers: Superior, Kyle Tack, Donn Leson, Martin, Roohide, Bob’s Custom. Look for the maker’s stamp on the fender. A legitimate competition saddle holds its value and fit.
