Understanding Reining Saddles

What to Look For in a Used Reining Saddle

Reining saddles are built for precision pattern work — flat seat, minimal horn, in-skirt rigging, round skirts. The best used reining saddles come from competition-proven makers: Bob's Custom, Kyle Tack, Donn Leson, Superior Saddlery, Martin Saddlery, and Roohide. Look for the maker's stamp on the fender. A legitimate competition saddle from a known maker holds value and holds its fit.
