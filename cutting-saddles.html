Understanding Cutting Saddles

The Cutting Saddle Difference

Deep seat, high cantle, tall horn, dropped rigging — every feature exists because of NCHA’s free-rein rule. Once a competitor drops the reins, the saddle must hold them passive through violent lateral moves. A Teddy Johnson with NCHA competition history is a proven tool.

Makers David Recommends
