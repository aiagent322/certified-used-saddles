Understanding Ranch Saddles

Working Ranch vs. AQHA Ranch Riding

The SRS Ranch Saddle at $1,495 is a working ranch tool — back cinch, fully rigged, ready for cattle work. The Superior Ranch Rider builds ($5,495) are show-quality competition saddles for AQHA Ranch Riding competition. Both are legitimate ranch saddles. Know which you need before you buy.
