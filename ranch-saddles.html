Understanding Ranch Saddles

Working Ranch vs. AQHA Ranch Riding

The SRS Ranch Saddle at $1,495 is a working ranch tool — fully equipped with back cinch and billets, hand tooled, ready for actual cattle work. The Superior Ranch Rider builds ($5,495) are show-quality competition saddles built for AQHA Ranch Riding — elephant-print seats, #1 flower tooling, show presentation. Both are legitimate ranch saddles. Know which you need before you buy.

Ranch Riding is the Fastest Growing AQHA Event

AQHA Ranch Riding requires a stock saddle with visible horn and back cinch. The Superior Ranch Rider models in this inventory present correctly for competition while delivering the build quality AQHA judges expect. At 16½ inches these saddles fit a broad range of adult riders.
