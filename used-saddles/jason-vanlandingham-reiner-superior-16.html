Jason Vanlandingham Reiner by Superior Saddlery — 16" Bison Seat

$2,995

The Jason Vanlandingham signature reiner by Superior Saddlery is one of Andy Mashke's most recognizable builds — and the defining feature of this example is immediately visible: bison leather covering both the seat and the swell and horn, producing a distinctive pebbly texture and rich dark espresso tone that sets it apart from the smooth-leather builds in the lineup. Bison leather is prized for its durability and break-in characteristics, becoming suppler and more comfortable with each ride.

The dark espresso leather throughout creates a moody, sophisticated look that pairs well with the oxidized silver round conchos on the skirts and fenders. The AM Superior Saddlery badge on the fender is clearly visible and confirms the build. The saddle has been honestly used and presents in good pre-owned condition — the bison leather has developed a natural patina that adds character rather than detracting from it.

At $2,995 plus shipping, this is one of the most accessible entry points into a name-brand Superior Saddlery competition reiner. Jason Vanlandingham is a multiple NRHA Futurity finalist with one of the most recognizable names in the sport. Contact David Solum for a shipping quote to your location.
