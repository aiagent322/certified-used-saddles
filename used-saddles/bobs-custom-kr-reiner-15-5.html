The KR Reiner model from Bob's Custom Saddle is a purpose-built NRHA competition platform — designed with the seat geometry, ground seat, and swells that serious reiners demand. Serial B22-453M places this in Bob's Custom's 2022 production run. The 15½" dark chocolate smooth-out seat fits a wide range of riders and shows the hallmark comfort shaping Bob's Custom is known for.

Full hand-tooled floral leather covers the skirts, fenders, and jockeys — a rich, deep pattern that shows beautifully against the dark chocolate tones. The silver package includes sterling silver square conchos at the front jockey and rear, with sterling silver rear dees that add both function and flash. The Bob's Custom maker badge is clearly visible on the front rigging — a mark of quality recognized throughout NRHA competition.
