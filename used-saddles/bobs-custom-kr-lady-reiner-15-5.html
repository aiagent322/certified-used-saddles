Bob's Custom KR Lady Reiner — 15½" Bison Seat

$4,995

The Bob's Custom KR Lady Reiner in model B21-584 is a purpose-built competition saddle for smaller-framed riders and women competitors — and the bison leather seat is its defining feature. The dark black bison seat and swell/horn covering produces the same pebbly texture and durability characteristics that serious riders prize, breaking in progressively with each ride while maintaining its shape and grip. The AM Signature Series designation confirms this is a premium-tier Bob's Custom build.

The underlying leatherwork combines tight basketweave on the horn, swell, and fender panels with deep hand-carved floral scrollwork on the skirts, jockeys, and borders — a two-tone look produced entirely in the carving rather than in the leather color. Oxidized silver conchos on the fenders and skirts give the saddle a traditional, understated presence that doesn't compete with the leatherwork. The Bob's Custom badge is clearly visible on the fender.

The 15½" seat is ideal for the woman competitive rider or anyone seeking a performance-fit seat. At $4,995 plus shipping, this is a rare opportunity to acquire a Bob's Custom KR Lady Reiner with the bison seat upgrade in excellent pre-owned condition.
