Bob's Custom Saddle — 16" Seat — Tan Leather, Sterling Silver

$5,995

This Bob's Custom Saddle stands out from the crowd with a warm, light tan leather that shows the handtooling to exceptional advantage — the basketweave pattern on the horn, swell, and fenders contrasts beautifully against the deep hand-carved floral medallions and scrollwork on the skirts and jockeys. The color combination is brighter and more traditional than the dark-oil builds, with the tan leather catching light in a way that makes the carving depth look even more pronounced.

The silver package is all bright sterling — not oxidized — with corner plates framing both the front and rear skirts, raised floral appliqué sprays along the skirt borders, and sterling conchos and dee rings throughout. The signature five-flower stud line along the cantle arch is a classic Bob's Custom detail visible in the rear photo. The Bob's Custom badge on the fender confirms the build. Great pre-owned condition throughout — leather supple and even, silver bright, tree solid.

Priced at $5,995 plus shipping. Two distinct Bob's Custom 16" builds are available in inventory — compare this tan leather/bright sterling build against the darker basketweave/oxidized silver listing to find the look that fits your pen. Contact David Solum for a shipping quote to your location.

Shipping available. David ships nationwide. Contact for a freight quote to your zip.
